About Me#

Eagle Dad / Girl Dad / First Responder / Hacker Nerd / Aspiring Polymath

Open Source, Linux, Terminal, Bash, Tmux, and NeoVim, are the way. My homelab is my safe space.

This is my attempt at a blog to share what I learn with anyone who may be interested.

If you’re nerd and have questions, ideas, or just want to connect, contact me on LinkedIn!

I enjoy using technology to solve complex problems. I explore and discover new tips tools and techniques. I record the struggled and successes of my efforts in notes or wiki pages. Building on previous efforts and sharing with others is very rewarding.

There are many YouTube channels and Blogs that inspire and guide me. I read a LinkedIn post by John Noll that inspired me to start this blog. I will be attempting the 30 day learn out loud challenge. Thank you Josh

The 30 day LearnOutLoud challenge rules.

  1. Write about something you learned,
    or something that’s on your mind
    every day for 30 days.
  2. It has to be written publicly.
    Your blog, Medium, Substack, LinkedIn…
    whatever works for you.
  3. If you miss a day, you start over.

Be brave enough to fail at something new.
